Frequently Asked Questions

What is blockchain money transfer?
It uses decentralized networks (like Ethereum or Solana) to move funds securely peer-to-peer, bypassing traditional banking intermediaries. This often results in lower fees and faster settlement times.
How long does a transaction take?
Depending on gas fees selected, it usually takes between 10 seconds (Fast/Instant) to 5 minutes (Slow). Network congestion can occasionally impact these times.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We use military-grade AES-256 encryption for personal data and non-custodial wallet connections, meaning we never have direct access to your private keys.
